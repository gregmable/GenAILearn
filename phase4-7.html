Topic Guide

How the Platform GenAI Portfolio Fits Together

the platform's GenAI portfolio is strongest when you see it as a set of complementary capabilities mapped to different moments in the delivery lifecycle. Blueprint accelerates early design. Autopilot assists build-time authoring. Knowledge Buddy supports knowledge retrieval. GenAI Connect and related integration components connect workflows to models and tools. AgentX adds multi-agent orchestration for more complex scenarios. Each product addresses a different point in the path from idea to production execution.

This matters because enterprise teams often buy or prototype isolated AI tools that never connect to real business outcomes. the platform's advantage is that its AI features are embedded in an end-to-end workflow platform. That means summarization, drafting, search, recommendation, routing, and human review can all happen in the same governed process. The portfolio is not just a list of features; it is an operating model for shipping AI inside business applications.

  • Blueprint helps teams go from idea to initial solution design faster.
  • Autopilot accelerates app authoring and rule generation during build.
  • Knowledge Buddy delivers grounded enterprise answers through RAG.
  • AgentX and Connect extend those capabilities into orchestrated runtime workflows.
