Topic Guide

Why Pega's Positioning Matters

Pega's strategy is useful for enterprises because it anchors AI in real workflow context. Instead of asking whether a model can answer a question in isolation, the platform asks where AI should assist, where deterministic logic should remain in charge, and where a person must stay accountable.

This creates a practical operating model for adoption. Teams can map GenAI features to business steps, approval points, and service outcomes instead of treating AI as a separate experiment running beside the actual application stack.

Beginner Foundation

Introduction to Pega GenAI and the Use Case

What is Pega GenAI?

Pega GenAI is a suite of features that embed generative AI into Pega applications. It allows applications to "understand" and act on text instructions and to generate content or suggestions automatically.

Key capabilities:

  • Used at design time (building) and run time (operations)
  • Flexible, intelligent helper for users
  • Automates repetitive tasks
  • Provides conversational guidance in natural language
  • Adapts to complex or new situations on the fly

Why is Pega GenAI Needed?

Traditional business apps require users to click through forms and follow static flows. Pega GenAI adds a more flexible, intelligent helper:

  • Automate repetitive tasks: Filling fields, sending responses, classifying cases
  • Provide conversational guidance: Users ask in natural language instead of searching menus
  • Adapt to new situations: Adjust processes or suggest actions when unusual cases arise

Result: Users spend less time on manual work and more time on high-value decisions.

The Insurance Claim Scenario

Throughout this course, we follow a real-world example: Acme Insurance processing a car accident claim. Without Pega GenAI, the adjuster manually reviews all details. With GenAI enabled:

  • An AI Agent automatically reads claim details and generates a summary
  • A GenAI Coach suggests next steps ("Verify the police report is attached")
  • An AI Assistant chat answers questions about policy coverage
  • Tools fetch additional data or perform actions automatically
  • MCP invokes external AI services (like fraud detection)

Key Insight: Pega GenAI integrates multiple AI capabilities to make a claim handler's job faster, more confident, and more accurate. Each component (Agent, Tools, Assistant, Coach, MCP) plays a specific role in the workflow.
