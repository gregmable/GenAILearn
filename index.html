Site Overview

What This Site Is For

This site is a structured learning portal for building enterprise GenAI capability from fundamentals to production operations. It is designed for teams who need a practical, phased path that combines platform-neutral AI concepts with a dedicated Pega track.

What It Includes
  • 4 learning phases with objective-based pages.
  • Phase quizzes with progression and unlock rules.
  • Resources and glossary pages for reference.
How To Use It
  • Start in Phase 1 and move sequentially.
  • Use quizzes to validate understanding per phase.
  • Use Phase 4 for all Pega-specific deep dives.
Audience
  • Architects and developers implementing GenAI.
  • Product and process teams planning AI rollout.
  • Enablement leads building enterprise capability.
