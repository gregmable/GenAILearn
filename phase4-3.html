What Still Requires Architecture Judgment

Pega reduces implementation friction, but it does not remove architectural decisions. Teams still have to define source-of-truth documents, freshness, ownership, access enforcement, fallback behavior, and how low-confidence results should be handled.

The best implementations treat the knowledge service as a living operational system with evaluation criteria, index refresh policy, and explicit business owners for the indexed content.

User Support & Information

AI Assistant – Instant Answers and Guidance

What is an AI Assistant in Pega?

Beyond case-focused Agents, Pega GenAI offers a more general AI Assistant experience—a conversational Q&A helper that can fetch and synthesize information for users on the fly.

Example: Pega's Knowledge Buddy is an AI-powered chatbot that can answer questions by drawing from the company's knowledge bases and documents.

An AI Assistant excels at:

  • Providing information and explanations
  • Drafting content on request
  • Synthesizing data from multiple sources

Why Have an AI Assistant?

Organizations have tons of information (policies, manuals, product details) that are hard to search manually. An AI Assistant can:

  • Instantly retrieve and summarize relevant information
  • Maintain consistency — everyone gets the same vetted answer
  • Cite sources for credibility and trust
  • Support both employees and customers with self-service options

Result: Users find what they need quickly and confidently.

Real-World Scenario: Knowledge Buddy in Action

Adjuster: "Is there any state regulation about rental car coverage for accidents?"

Knowledge Buddy: "Yes, in this state the insurer must provide up to 10 days of rental car coverage while the claim is processed. (Source: State Regulations Document 2024)"

The adjuster didn't have to dig through manuals or call a supervisor. The Assistant searched the knowledge base and returned a concise, cited answer.
