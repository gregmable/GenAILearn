Operating Model

Readiness Checklist for Pega AI Rollout

  • Define which actions are autonomous, assistive, or always human-approved.
  • Log model usage, retrieval context, prompts, and downstream actions for audit.
  • Establish incident response for weak answers, outages, permissions drift, and unsafe tool calls.
  • Assign ownership for prompts, models, indexed content, and workflow outcomes.
Topic Guide

Production Readiness Is a Workflow Discipline

Pega deployments succeed when AI operations are treated like workflow operations. Monitoring, approvals, incident handling, and runbooks need to be just as explicit for AI-enabled work as they already are for core business process automation.

The key is proportional control: lightweight assistants can move quickly, but anything that changes decisions, communications, or downstream systems needs stronger governance and clear evidence trails.
