Guided User Experience

GenAI Coach – Step-by-Step Guidance

What is Pega GenAI Coach?

GenAI Coach gives users real-time guided assistance during complex workflows. If an AI Assistant is open-ended Q&A, GenAI Coach is more structured—like a built-in coach or mentor.

Key difference: Coach provides contextual tips, checklists, or answers based on predefined prompts, ensuring guidance is consistent and on-point.

The Coach appears in the UI to guide users step-by-step, often presenting a menu of helpful questions rather than free-form input.

Why Do We Have GenAI Coach?

Not every user is an expert, and some processes can be tricky. The GenAI Coach aims to "make every employee your best employee" by providing:

  • Consistent, role-specific help without needing an expert nearby
  • Control through curation: Organization pre-defines which questions are available
  • On-demand expertise that improves productivity and confidence
  • Targeted insights exactly when needed in the workflow

Real-World Scenario: Coach Guiding an Adjuster

A relatively new adjuster opens a claim. The GenAI Coach panel appears with guidance options:

Coach: "How can I help you with this claim?"

Adjuster clicks: "Are all required documents present?"

Coach: "All key documents are present except the police report. You might need to request that from the customer."

Adjuster then asks: "What's the next step in the process?"

Coach: "Next, you should validate coverage and then proceed to damage appraisal. I can assist with starting the appraisal if needed."

This context-aware, guided experience makes complex workflows feel less daunting, ensuring even a novice adjuster can follow best practices.

Topic Guide

Protect the Abstraction Boundaries

Pega architectures stay maintainable when model choice is abstracted behind stable integration contracts. That lets teams change providers, gateway policies, or routing rules without rewriting the entire workflow experience.

The practical goal is to keep user-facing journeys stable while allowing controlled change behind the integration and foundation layers.
